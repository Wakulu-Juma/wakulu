Theme

This year's convocation has selected a topic "The role of official statistics and big data in the economic transformations and suitable development of Tanzania" as the standing point to reflect on the theme of this year's 18 of November African Statistics day. The theme states "Modernizing data ecosystems to accelerate the implementation of African Continent Free Trade Area (AfCFTA): The role of official Statistics in the economic transformation and suitable development of Africa"
